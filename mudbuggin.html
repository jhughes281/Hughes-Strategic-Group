About Mudbuggin Seafood

Mudbuggin Seafood is our seafood catering venture, delivering boiled crawfish, shrimp, crab, chargrilled and raw oysters, and classic Southern sides for private parties, corporate events, and community gatherings. Everything is made fresh in the Gulf Coast tradition and seasoned with our own Cajun-Creole blend.
